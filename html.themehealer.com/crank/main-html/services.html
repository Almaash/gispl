e-Sampadaa

An effective tracking & monitoring system to monitor and make grading system for Stakeholders. Monitoring of the entire items supplied from single unified portal. Automatic alerts & notifications for items approaching expiry. Single as well as Bulk Raising of complaints with the respective suppliers. Real time progress report at a glance. Effective IEC and communication with Users. Better data analysis for preparation of Procurement action plans.

e-PMS

End-to-end project management and performance monitoring system for government and municipal initiatives, ensuring efficiency and accountability in all projects.

AttendanceTracker

A Web & Mobile based Application which keeps record of Employees working hours & Presence at work. It Marks Employees Attendance, Leaves, On & Off working Hours. Tamper proof & Secured Solution which provides accurate data. Efficient tool to manage & monitor the activities of Safai Karmcharies & Field staff. It helps to calculate the wages as per the working hours. Detailed information is available on fingertips of the labour engaged by any Agency.

SmartPark

IoT-powered parking management solution to optimize urban mobility, reduce congestion, and increase municipal revenue with smart parking sensors and real-time monitoring dashboards.

HoardingTracker

Geo-tagging and survey of all hoardings in the jurisdiction of the Municipal body using a mobile app. To improve revenue collection by detecting illegal hoardings. Establishing a strong link between the geographical location of hoardings on Google Maps and its related data. Android & Web apps allow monitoring, updating, and citizen complaints.

SONAR

SONAR comes as a SAAS (Software-as-a-Service) security solution. It protects against dynamic threats and fraudulent websites. Works 24x7 with alerts and notifications. Intuitive dashboard for monitoring threats daily, weekly, and monthly. Independent cloud solution without disturbing existing website or hosting setup.

Public data stored on decentralized Blockchain for full transparency, zero manipulation, multi-team usage, high security, and zero backups.
